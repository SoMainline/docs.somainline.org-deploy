MT6795 (Helio-X10)
===============================

Built on a 28nm process, this SoC has a "decent" CPU compartment with 8xCortex-A53 reaching a maximum frequency of 2.2GHz, and a good PowerVR-G6200 GPU with a maximum frequency of 700MHz; competes with MSM8992.
